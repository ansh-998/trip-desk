# Decision log

Keep this updated as you build. It is not for submission, but it makes the
README's "decisions I'm proud of" section and the one-hour interview much
easier, since the reasoning will still be fresh.

Suggested format per entry: what the brief left open, what you chose, why,
and what you gave up.

## Example

**Open question:** the brief does not say what happens to a lead when its
trip closes.

**Decision:** the lead stays visible in admin (so the team can still follow
up or mark it NOT_A_FIT), it just disappears from the public page.

**Why:** losing a lead's history because a trip filled up felt like exactly
the kind of crack the brief is trying to fix.

**Trade-off:** none significant at this scope.
